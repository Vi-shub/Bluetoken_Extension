# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in BlueToken a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:**

- Being respectful and constructive
- Focusing on what is best for the community and the project
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully

**Examples of unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others’ private information
- Shaming people for using AI tools (this project is about awareness, not blame)

## Enforcement

Report issues to the maintainer via the contact options on [shubhamvishwakarma.com](https://www.shubhamvishwakarma.com). Maintainers may remove content or ban contributors who violate this code.
